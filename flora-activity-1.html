Sprouting Avocado Seeds

Avocado seeds are usually composted or thrown away but after eating you can grow the seed. This wonâ€™t make any fruit but will make a nice plant.

    You will need:
  • An avocado seed
  • Three toothpicks
  • A cup or vase (clear is best so you can see it grow)
  • Water
  • Patience (it might take many months to grow!)
    Steps:
  1. Clean the avocado seed and find which end is pointy
  2. Stick the toothpicks into the side of the seed leaning towards the pointy end of the seed
  3. Place the seed and toothpicks on the cup with the pointy end upwards
  4. Fill the cup with water until the seed is half underwater
  5. Watch the seed sprout and grow
  6. Plant the seed in a pot when the sprout is about 15cm tall
